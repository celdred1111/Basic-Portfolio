About Me

I am Chris Eldred. I am a Tri-National. I am currently legally permitted to work in the United States, the European Union, Britain and the British Commonwealth. I am fluent in English, Spanish and Italian. I am work proficient in French, and Sinti, as well as the basics of Russian, Ukrainian, and Arabic. My interests are poetry and art in the style of magical realism, as well as, soccer, rugby, boxing. In particular, I adore Neruda and el Gabo, Tottenham, the London Irish, Vasyl Lomachenko and Josh Kelly.
